In the News

Coronavirus Isn’t Going Anywhere, How Do We Learn to Live With It?

After a tumultuous and unprecedented year that turned our lives upside down, 2021 was welcomed with open arms. There was hope that the year would bring with it a return to normalcy - and take us back to life as we knew it before the COVID-19 pandemic first struck us.

With major advancements in vaccines, falling cases and a reduced death rate in a majority of regions, this expectation, at first, didn’t appear to be far-off. But a deeper look into the way viruses generally work, combined with what we know so far about SARS-CoV-2, reveals that the coronavirus is here to stay - perhaps for all times to come.
