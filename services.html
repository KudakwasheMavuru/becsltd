We also Supply and maintain Computerized Maintenance Management Software (CMMS) for their maintenance departments.



A system software that centralizes Maintenance Information and translates the process of maintenance. It simplifies maintenance management and help organizations reduce maintenance costs and extend equipment life span.

One good example is for a completed Work Order

The system software links all elements of repair and maintenance work and record them in one place/database as follows:


  • What was required?
  • Who did the work?
  • What is the location of the equipment?
  • What parts were used?
  • What is the cost of the used parts?
  • When the work was assigned, initiated and completed?
  • What was the outcome?
  • Where was the work done?
  • What was the problem or preventive action?

CHECKS IF YOUR ORGANIZATION NEEDS CMMS.

If you are considering taking on a CMMS implementation, the goals and vision of the maintenance department are important. The following questions must be considered:


  • When was the last time the equipment was worked on and what was the problem?
  • Where the spare part was bought from and contact details?
  • Who are the technicians who have worked on the equipment?
  • What is the location and position of the equipment?
  • Is there a substitute spare part in the storeroom?
  • Is the equipment under warranty? Have we performed the required warranty maintenance?
  • What is the serial number and model?
  • What was the cost of repairs? Should we replace the equipment?
  • When was the last time a spare part was used? Is it obsolete?
  • How much have spent on Preventive Maintenance?
